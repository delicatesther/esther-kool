The NOS is the Dutch Broadcasting Foundation, governmentally funded to broadcast impartial news and cover sports-events.

At NOS I work as a software engineer at the digital media department. I used to be part of the team covering the digital channels of Jeugdjournaal, NOS op 3, NOS Stories and Teletekst. Now, I maintain and develop the websites for the NOS and Nieuwsuur.
