Under guidance of a senior front-end developer I helped redesign Sundio's website elizawashere.nl. Eliza Was Here was part of a range of websites under Sundio that all had the same backend structure and templates. This meant that styling often meant we couldn't adjust any HTML and could only apply our CSS based on the elements already on the page. This called for some very creative solutions.
