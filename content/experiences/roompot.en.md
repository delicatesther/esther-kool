From 2016 onward I was responsible for the maintenance of the front-end for roompot.nl, vakantiegevoel.nl and their park-specific local sites. This entailed adding new features, cleaning up technical debt and implementing A/B tests. In 2019 I also set up a completely new mobile-only platform (which is not available live).

Both the old- and new platforms were based on CMS Episerver. While my teammates focused on the C#.NET backend, I was responsible for the entire FE. Our Roompot team fully operated based on scrum/agile.

### Technology

- HTML5, 
- SASS/CSS3, 
- jQuery, 
- Vue, 
- NPM, 
- Webpack, 
- NPM CLI, 
- C#.NET, 
- EpiServer
