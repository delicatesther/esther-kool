For a few months I was responsible for the FE development of OMRON Healthcare UK/NL/DE, their B2C webshop.

This entailed optimising existing features. One of the most important things I delivered was the promo page for their HeartGuid, a watch that also functions as a bloodpressure tracker.

OMRON was part of a multi-client scrum/agile project team. Tasks were divided among developers based on availability. I was FE lead for OMRON and Paula's Choice in this setting.
